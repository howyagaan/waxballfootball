2025 Season Notes

A few end-of-year stats and storylines pulled from the full weekly Waxball archive.

Best weekly score 179.94

Paul Legallet recorded the highest single-week score of the regular season in Week 6. He also had the third-highest weekly score with 171.58 in Week 4.

Lowest weekly score 61.14

Jacob Moskovitz had the lowest single-week score in Week 11, then recovered late, made the playoffs, and finished 3rd overall.

Regular-season heartbreak 0.02 pts

Travis Rogers beat Nicholas Hamilton by 0.02 points in Week 3, the closest regular-season matchup of 2025.

Biggest blowout +85.18

Erik Ohno Dagoberg beat Travis Rogers by 85.18 points in Week 10, the largest regular-season margin.

Top PF pace Miles Blue

Miles Blue finished with the league's best points-per-game pace, edging Paul Legallet by 0.25 points per game.

First-place curse 2 years

For the second straight season, the regular-season leader failed to win a playoff game. In 2025, Paul Legallet finished 4th.

Streak kings 5 games

Milo Manheim and Erik Ohno Dagoberg each had five-game winning streaks. Jacob Moskovitz had the season's longest losing streak, also five games.

Late escape 1-5 to safe

Travis Roy Rogers started 1-5 and spent four weeks in last place, but survived the Toilet Bowl by beating Jakob Cooper in Week 17.
